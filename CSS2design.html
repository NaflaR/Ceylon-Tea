    Grades of Tea


    Ceylon tea is classified into different classes. These grade names are an indicator of the size or appearance of the leaf produced and not of its consistency.


    • BOP - A well-made, neat medium-sized leaf with no extra stalk or fabric. There should be no fine particles (fannings and dust) that are not true-to-grade.

    • BOP Sp - Larger in size than a BOP lack and clean in appearance. Note: BOP & BOP special be treated as two grades, but for cataloging purposes treat as one grade.

    • BOPF - Neat leaf, pretty clean. Although smaller than the BOP class. No fine dust should be present.

    • BOP 1 - It can be wired and bent, but shorter than OP1.

    • FBOP - Smaller / shorter than BOP1 with tips, but larger than FBOPF1.

    • FBOP 1 - Large, bent, wiry leaf. It's pretty tippy. It's longer than BOP1.

    • PEKOE - Shot, curly or semi-cirly leaves of any size.

    • PEKOE1 - Same as Pekoe, but smaller in size than Pekoe of any elevation. This replaces the Flowery Pekoe grade. Note: Pekoe and Fekoe 1 will be treated as two grades, but for cataloguing purposes treat as one grade.

    • FBOPF (FF) - Similar in size to BOP, which must have tips.

    • FBOPF 1 (FF1) - Longer than BOP. Smaller than the FBOP with a tip show.

    • FBOPF - The same scale as BOP with a decent presence of tips.

    • FBOPF Ex. Sp. - Tiny leaf which must have an enticing display with golden or silver tips with a small black leaf.

    • FBOPF Ex. Sp1 - Leafy and must have an enticing display with golden or silver tips with a small black leaf.

    • OP 1 - Long, well wired or partially twisted.

    • OP - Less wiry than OP1, but more distorted than OPA.

    • OPA - Long bold leaf tea with a twist of breath.

    • BP - (Off Grades) - The hard, hard leaf should be choppy.

    • BOP 1A - (Off Grades) - Any flak leaf with no stalk or fiber (Clean Tea).

    • BM (BROKENS) - (Off Grades) Blended leaf tea. It may have more fiber and stalk than BOP 1A.

    • BT - (Off Grades) - All blended teas of different volumes, with or without stalk and silk.
